FrogsWork WiFi Setup
====================

Ethernet (recommended)
  1. Plug the ethernet cable from your router into the FrogsWork box.
  2. Power on and wait 2 minutes.
  3. On your computer open http://frogswork.local

WiFi (this USB)
  1. On your Windows or Mac computer, open "WiFi Setup.html" on this USB.
  2. Enter your Wi-Fi network name and password, then save.
  3. Safely eject the USB and plug it into the powered-on FrogsWork box.
  4. Wait 2 minutes, then open frogswork-setup.log on this USB if anything failed.
  5. Remove the USB, join the same Wi-Fi on your computer, open http://frogswork.local

Support: see the quick-start card in your box.
