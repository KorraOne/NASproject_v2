FrogsWork WiFi Setup

Use this when you cannot plug an ethernet cable into your router.

What happens next
  1. Save your Wi-Fi details to this USB (below).
  2. Safely eject the USB from your computer.
  3. Plug the USB into your powered-on FrogsWork box.
  4. Wait about 2 minutes, then plug the USB back into your computer.
  5. Open frogswork-setup.log on the USB — look for Finished: SUCCESS.
  6. Join the same Wi-Fi on your computer and open http://frogswork.local
Ethernet is easier — if you can run a cable from the box to your router, use ethernet instead of this USB.
